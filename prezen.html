#Directed Analysis

I hate Outlook. I think it is poorly designed and incredibly frustrating. In these series of images, I have overlaid the "Rule of Thirds" grid described in Chapter 6 of Presentation Zen. I love rules and structure, and while designs are meant to intentionally break these rules, Outlook is horrible at breaking them impactfully. Nothing aligns with the visual appeal of the grids, I tried to cut Microsoft some slack by realigning the grid to where to content starts, but nothing is centered within a box, the subjects of the screen do not align in a box or powerpoint. They are either content that is painfully oversized or the icons are misaligned, unequally sized, or repetitive.
