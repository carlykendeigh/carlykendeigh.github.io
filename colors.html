#Approximate Analogy

The way color is described in The Complete Color Harmony is very similar to how sound operates. In high school, my choir director always compared sound to color; which isn't a far compatison. The notes are hues (red or yellow to A or C), octaves are value (light and dark vs high and low), intensity/saturation is the volume (dynamics).
